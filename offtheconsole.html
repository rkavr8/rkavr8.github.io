Games Are Fun Even Off the Console!

Playing games on a console, PC, or tablet is fun but can also lead to eye strain. It's always a good idea to take time to play something offline. Here are some of our favorite games to play.

  • Throw Throw Burrito
  • Bottle Flip
  • Uno

Game Why We Love It
Throw Throw Burrito Dodge ball, but with burritos!
Bottle Flip Free and can be played anywhere!
Uno Easy, Fun, and a Classic!

Throw Throw Burrito

The world's first dodgeball card game! It combines our favorite activities - playing cards and throwing things at each other. This game can be played with players of all ages and can go from an organized card game to mass chaos in minutes.

Bottle Flip

Bottle flip is a classic game that can be played anywhere! We've even played it around the world at Epcot! All you need is a mostly empty bottle and patience. It's a game for all ages and you can invent challenges to keep it interesting.

Uno

Uno is a classic. It's easy to play and fun for everyone. Plus - Uno makes a lot of really fun themed decks - like the Legend of Zelda!!!
